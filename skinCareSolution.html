FAQs

Is your pharmacy registered and safe to use in the UK?

Yes, we are fully licensed by the General Pharmaceutical Council (GPhC) and operate under UK regulations. All consultations and dispensing are handled by qualified professionals based in Leeds.

Do I need a prescription to order medication from your website?

Some medications require a prescription. You can complete a short online consultation, and our in-house prescribers will assess whether the treatment is appropriate and safe for you.

How quickly can I receive my order if I live in Leeds?

For customers in Leeds, we offer fast local delivery and collection options.

Do you deliver outside Leeds or West Yorkshire?

Currently, we primarily serve the Leeds area and its surrounding regions. If you’re outside this region, please contact us before placing an order to confirm whether we can deliver.

Is my information safe when using your online service?

Yes, your medical and personal data are securely stored and fully protected. We use encrypted systems to keep your details confidential.

Can I speak to a pharmacist in person or by phone?

Yes. You can speak to a pharmacist at our Leeds location or get advice over the phone.

Do you accept NHS prescriptions at your pharmacy?

Yes, we are an NHS-contracted pharmacy and can dispense NHS prescriptions. You can also access private treatments.

Is the medication you supply genuine and UK-approved?

Yes, all medications are sourced from MHRA-approved UK suppliers and dispensed from our GPhC-registered pharmacy in Leeds.

What happens if I experience side effects or issues with my medication?

If you experience side effects, you should contact our pharmacy team immediately. We offer aftercare support and will help you decide the best next steps.

Can I collect my medication in person from your Leeds pharmacy?

Yes. If you prefer, you can choose to collect your order from our pharmacy directly. Just select the collection option at checkout, and we’ll notify you when it’s ready.
